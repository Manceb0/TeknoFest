# Product

## Register

product

## Users

TEKNOFEST judges, project presenters, and road-safety operators using the system during a live demonstration. Their primary task is to start a video session, understand detections immediately, and inspect the evidence behind a high-risk event.

## Product Purpose

QuisMotion demonstrates an end-to-end Edge AI road-safety workflow: verified access, interchangeable video sources, real-time detection telemetry, proximity-triggered QoD, incident persistence, diagnostics, and a credible path from mock inference to Roboflow-trained models.

## Brand Personality

Technical, composed, and alert. It should feel like a focused field instrument made by a capable engineering team, with enough visual identity to be memorable in a competition setting.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic neon cyberpunk dashboards, decorative sci-fi HUD clutter, marketing-style metric walls, and interfaces whose visual effects compete with the video evidence.

## Design Principles

- Keep the video and current safety state as the visual priority.
- Explain every automated decision with visible signals and evidence.
- Make demo recovery obvious through manual controls and clear connection states.
- Treat mock and real providers honestly and label them at all times.
- Support new media and inference providers through replaceable adapters.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.1 AA contrast, keyboard-accessible controls, visible focus states, redundant text and color status cues, and reduced-motion support.
